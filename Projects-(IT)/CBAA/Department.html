Department

Business Administration

  • Goal

    To produce business leaders equipped with versatile skills and ethical practices for global markets.

  • Vision

    Empower students with comprehensive knowledge in various business disciplines, fostering critical thinking, innovation, and effective decision-making through experiential learning and industry engagement.

  • Mission

    To be a renowned institution shaping future business administrators who drive sustainable organizational growth, embrace diversity, and pioneer transformative business practices.

  • Objectives

    1. Provide a curriculum encompassing diverse areas like marketing, finance, operations, and human resources.
    2. Cultivate leadership qualities and entrepreneurship skills among students through practical experiences and mentorship programs.
    3. Foster a collaborative learning environment that encourages creativity, critical analysis, and adaptability to dynamic business landscapes.
    4. Establish partnerships with industry leaders to facilitate internships, workshops, and real-world projects that bridge theory and practice.
    5. Instill a strong sense of ethical values and social responsibility in future business leaders.

Accountancy

  • Goal

    To produce competent and ethical accountants capable of navigating complex financial landscapes with integrity.

  • Vision

    Provide a comprehensive education in accounting principles, practices, and regulations while nurturing ethical values, analytical skills, and professional integrity to excel in the field of accountancy.

  • Mission

    To be a distinguished institution recognized for producing globally competitive accountants who uphold transparency, accuracy, and ethical standards in financial reporting and decision-making.

  • Objectives

    1. Offer a rigorous and up-to-date curriculum covering various accounting domains including auditing, taxation, financial reporting, and forensic accounting.
    2. Prepare students to pass professional accounting examinations and obtain relevant certifications (e.g., CPA, CMA) by providing comprehensive exam preparation and support.
    3. Provide experiential learning opportunities through internships, case studies, and simulated scenarios to bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application.
    4. Foster a culture of integrity, ethics, and accountability in handling financial information and decision-making.
    5. Engage with professional accounting bodies, industry experts, and alumni networks to offer insights, mentorship, and networking opportunities for students pursuing a career in accountancy.
