Degree Programs Offered

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

This program equips students with essential knowledge, skills, and attitudes in providing crucial financial information relevant to management decisions. Students are trained in devising, planning, and expertise in financial reporting, performance management systems, and the formulation of organizational policies, procedures, and strategies.

Click for more details
  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum Highlights: Covers financial reporting, performance management, organizational policies, and strategies.
  • Career Opportunities: Positions in accounting firms, corporations, financial institutions, and government agencies.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

This program focuses on the interrelationships among different functional areas of business, such as economics, human resource management, and marketing. It helps students develop critical and analytical thinking, human relations, communication, quantitative, and technical skills needed for leadership roles in both public and private sectors.

Click for more details
  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum Highlights: Includes economics, human resource management, marketing management, and critical thinking skills.
  • Career Opportunities: Business leadership roles in various industries.

Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

This program emphasizes the importance and benefits of creating enterprises that contribute to economic and social development. It educates students on small business management, from idea generation to business plan preparation, providing hands-on experience in setting up and running a business venture.

Click for more details
  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum Highlights: Focuses on idea generation, business plan development, and hands-on business experience.
  • Career Opportunities: Entrepreneurship, small business ownership, innovation management.

Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting

Aims to produce globally competent graduates capable of applying accounting, financial, and business management principles while upholding professional values and ethics. Enables students to adapt to change throughout their professional lives.

Click for more details
  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum Highlights: Emphasizes accounting, financial management, and professional ethics.
  • Career Opportunities: Management accounting roles in various industries.
